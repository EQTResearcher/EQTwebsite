Observation as Participation: The Detection Theory of Energy Quantum Theory

Authors: Kaisheng Li, Longji Li

Status: Available / In Press

Abstract: This monograph establishes the Detection Theory of EQT, proposing that observation is not a passive recording of reality, but an active participation in the energy quantum density field $\rho(\mathbf{r}, t, f)$.
