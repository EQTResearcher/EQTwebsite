Planck's Unfinished Radiance: The Energy Quantum Theory and the Mass-Energy Unity Across Micro and Macro Scales

Authors: Kaisheng Li, Longji Li

Status: Available / In Press

Abstract: The true brilliance of Planck’s formula lies in its revelation of the mass-energy relationship in the microscopic world. Beyond photons, EQT unveils a neglected truth: by combining $E=h\nu$ with Einstein’s $E=mc^2$, we unlock the characteristic frequency of mass quanta:

$$m = \frac{h\nu}{c^2}$$

The bridge between frequency and inertia.

EQT emerges as a rediscovery of this truth, centering frequency to classify the universe into Energy Quanta (massless; photons, gravitons) and Mass Quanta (massive; electrons, Higgs), surpassing traditional boson-fermion classifications.

A "Frequency Map" of Reality

Imagine a map where particles are defined by their rhythmic signatures:

  • Gravitons: Low frequencies ($10^{-20}$ to $10^{3}$ Hz), sketching the contours of galaxies.
  • Photons: A vast electromagnetic spectrum freely traversing the cosmos.
  • Electrons: Marking the birth of matter at $10^{20}$ Hz.
  • Higgs Boson: The bridge at $10^{26}$ Hz, transforming energy into mass.
