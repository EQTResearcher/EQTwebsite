Authors: Kaisheng Li, Longji Li

Status: Available / In Press

Core Concepts and Fundamental Propositions of EQT

The "Energy Quanta Theory" (EQT) advocates using "frequency" and "energy density" as the primary language to describe nature, rather than a priori establishing "particles" or "fields" as fundamental entities. Key concepts include:

1. Energy Quantum (Energon): Not simply a photon or a strictly defined gauge boson, but a more primordial concept of a "discrete unit of energy," emphasizing an existence "anchored by frequency." The energy of each energon is quantified by $E = h\nu$, and its frequency $\nu$ determines its dynamics and coupling scale.
2. Mass Quantum (Masson): When energons become stationary ("reside") through non-linear feedback and phase-locking in a local region, they manifest as "inertia" and "rest mass." The masson is thus viewed as a frequency-condensed stable state.
3. Energy Quanta Density Field: Described by $\rho(\nu,x,t)$, this represents the number density of energons per unit volume and per unit frequency. Forces are determined by the gradient and time evolution of this field.

Fundamental Propositions

Proposition 1: On the Nature of Force
Force is not an independent exchange mediator, but a dynamic manifestation expressed during the process of the energy quanta density field tending toward equilibrium.
Mathematically: $f(x,t) \propto -\nabla\rho(x,t)$.

Proposition 2: On Mass Generation
The masson is a stationary state formed by the frequency condensation of high-frequency energons under non-linear coupling and feedback. This requires critical density and coupling strength conditions.

"This represents a 'Phenomenon-Mechanism-Mathematics' Trinity construction method."

Relationship with Traditional Concepts

EQT offers a more fundamental interpretation of the "particle-field" dichotomy:

Traditional Concept EQT Interpretation
Standard Model Bosons Stable manifestations of energon aggregation within specific frequency bands.
Higgs Mechanism Macroscopic expression of low-frequency condensation in the frequency spectrum.
Gravity & Dark Energy Collective effects of the extremely low-frequency energon density field.

In Summary: The "Energy Quantum" is not a heretical concept fundamentally opposed to current theories, but an attempt to establish a direct physical causal chain between the "phenomena" and the "mathematical structures" within existing theories.
